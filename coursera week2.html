Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Seed Pokémon. There is a plant seed on its back right from the day this Pokémon is born. The seed slowly grows larger.

Charmander

Charmander is a Fire type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Lizard Pokémon. It has a preference for hot things. When it rains, steam is said to spout from the tip of its tail.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Tiny Turtle Pokémon. When it retracts its long neck into its shell, it squirts out water with vigorous force.
